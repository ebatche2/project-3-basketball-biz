Basketball Overview

The NBA is a very entertaining and exciting league to watch. There are fans from all around the world and basketball has become a global sport. For our project we didn't want to focus on the game of basketball we wanted to focus on the business side of having your own NBA franchise. Many think that being an owner of a team means that you have lots of money and you can spend it as you please. There is more to being an owner then that.
